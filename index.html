I am currently a mobile app developer intern at North Manufacturing(Pvt) Ltd, and an undergraduate student at Vavuniya Campus of University of Jaffna, pursuing degree in Information Technology.

Growing up, I was always passionate about understanding how things worked, solving puzzles, and playing cricket. Until I was 15 I didn't have a computer and when I had one, I was trying to understand the logic behind how software interacted with hardware. At that time I already knew little bit about computer hardware, so natural curiosity led me to become who I am today. Then I had a habit of designing some posts for family, for friends using photoshop.

During my academic journey, I delved deeper into software development and uncovered the importance of not just writing code but understanding the larger picture of how software integrates with systems, how users interact with it, and how it adapts to change. I learned to approach problems with a systematic mindset, breaking them into manageable pieces and crafting efficient, scalable solutions. Beyond the technical aspects, software development taught me the value of collaboration, communication, and continuous learning.

Through software development, I’ve realized that it’s not just about building applications or tools but creating experiences that resonate with users and solve meaningful problems. Whether it's designing intuitive interfaces, optimizing performance, or architecting complex systems, the process of turning ideas into tangible solutions fuels my passion. I aspire to continue building software that not only meets technical expectations but also elevates the way we live, work, and interact with the world around us.
